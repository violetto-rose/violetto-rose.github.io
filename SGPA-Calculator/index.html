Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Processing

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This calculator operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No Server Uploads: All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript
  • No Data Storage: Your academic data, PDFs, and calculation results are never saved or stored anywhere
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any user behavior monitoring tools
  • No Third-Party Services: Your data never leaves your device or gets sent to external services

Local Storage & Technical Implementation

The only data we store locally on your device is:

  • Theme Preference: Whether you prefer light or dark mode (stored in browser localStorage)
  • Session Data: Temporary PDF extraction results during your session (automatically cleared when you close the browser)

Our privacy-first approach is built into the application architecture:

  • Client-Side Processing: PDF.js library processes documents entirely in your browser
  • No Network Requests: Apart from loading the application itself, no data is transmitted over the internet
  • Static Hosting: The application is hosted as static files with no server-side processing
  • Open Source: All code is transparent and can be audited on GitHub

Data Security

Your academic information remains secure because:

  • No Data Transmission: Your PDFs and grades never leave your device
  • Browser Security: Modern browser sandboxing protects your data from external access
  • No Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies or persistent identifiers
  • HTTPS Only: All connections are encrypted and secure

Bottom Line: Your academic data is completely private, secure, and never leaves your device. We have no access to your PDFs, grades, or personal information.
